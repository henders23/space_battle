The Line Breaks

HELION THEATRE · WAR CYCLE 47 · STATUS: CONTESTED

The Helion War is being lost.

For three years the Veyr Dominion has driven through the frontier, system by system, breaking every Commonwealth fleet sent to hold them. They do not think of themselves as conquerors. They look at a divided, hesitant Commonwealth and see only a civilisation that has earned the order they intend to impose.

And they are not alone. As the Navy falls back, frontier worlds that begged for protection and received speeches have begun to fly Dominion colours — the Helion Protectorate. Their ships are Commonwealth-built. Their captains trained in the same academies you did. When they hail you, they will not sound like the enemy.

The Greyward Line is collapsing. Your ship's captain is dead, and the Admiralty has run out of better options. Command of the frigate Resolute — old, patched, scarred long before you boarded her — now falls to you.

You are not here to win the war. Not yet. You are here to save what can be saved: ships, crews, civilians, and whatever is left of the line. In this war, a mission can succeed inside a defeat.

— Admiralty Command, Helion Front
